Osmania University will conduct selection trials for its cricket team that will participate in the HCA 2018-2019 two-day league at Marredpally Grounds here from 6am on Thursday and Friday. Colleges may field not more than two proven (HCA league, State and national) players for the trials. However, students from Nizam College will not be eligible to participate as it already has a separate team in the same league, according to a release from Prof Rajesh Kumar, Principal of University College of Physical Education (UCPE). Players should report at the venue to Dr R Harinarayana Rao and Mr Jaya Prakash along with certificates, paper clippings, eligibility forms and latest ID cards.
